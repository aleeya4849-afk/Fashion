Behind the Threads

Our mission to empower creativity in fashion

Empowering Creative Expression

Threaded by You was born from a simple idea: everyone should have the tools to express their unique style through fashion design.

Our platform brings together cutting-edge technology with timeless design principles, allowing you to create custom outfits that reflect your personality.

We believe that fashion should be accessible, sustainable, and personal. That's why we've created tools that make design intuitive and manufacturing efficient.
